How do I get to Chicago?

Depending on where you're located, probably flying into MDW or ORD. MDW is more south, near the University of Chicago, and a hub for Southwest. ORD is northwest of the city, and you can take the blue line straight to downtown from there. We advise checking flights into both airports to see the best ones from your home airport.

What should I do in Chicago?

There is so much to do! If you're coming from out-of-town, and you have the weekend to spare, we highly recommend taking the chance to explore. There are so many iconic landmarks, charming neighborhoods, and fun museums. This website is a great place to get started planning. We also always recommend the architecture boat tour.
